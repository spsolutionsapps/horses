What is Horses & Humans?

Horses & Humans is an international documentary program that explores the deep connection between people and horses across different cultures, territories and traditions.

Filmed in multiple countries around the world, each episode portrays the identity of a place through its equestrian culture, showcasing stories of tradition, work, sport and everyday life.

It is a platform for cultural visibility that positions countries, brands and institutions on a global stage through cinematic storytelling.
